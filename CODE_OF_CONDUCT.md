# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive environment for everyone. All participants in this project are expected to:

- **Be respectful and constructive.** Critique ideas, not people. Offer helpful feedback and assume good intent.
- **Be inclusive.** Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, and exclusionary behavior are not tolerated, regardless of experience level, identity, or background.
- **Focus on collaboration.** We are here to build something useful together. Stay on topic, help others when you can, and keep discussions productive.
- **Be honest.** If you make a mistake, own it. If you disagree, explain why with reasoning, not hostility.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or intimidation of any kind
- Discriminatory language or exclusionary jokes
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Sustained disruption of discussions or contributions
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all project spaces: GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other communication channels associated with this project.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, you can report it by:

- **GitHub**: Use GitHub's built-in reporting tools on the relevant issue, PR, or discussion

Reports will be reviewed promptly. We will maintain confidentiality to the extent possible.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate in response to violations, including warnings, temporary bans, or permanent removal from the project.
